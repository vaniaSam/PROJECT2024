Instructions

Single Player Mode Instructions:

  1. Click "Start" on the home screen.
  2. Choose single player.
  3. Flip cards to find matches.
  4. Match all before timer runs out to win.
  5. Click "Play again" to restart.

Multiplayer Mode Instructions:

  1. Click "Start" on the home screen.
  2. Choose multiplayer.
  3. Two players take turns flipping cards.
  4. Game ends when one player matches all.
  5. Winning player shown with "Winner".
  6. Click "Play again" to restart.
